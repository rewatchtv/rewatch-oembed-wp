A quick and easy way to enable the embedding of videos from your Rewatch channel.

== Description ==

Rewatch oEmbed enables embedding videos from your Rewatch channel within your Wordpress site.

Once the plugin is installed and activated, you can paste in the embed link from the video's page on your Rewatch channel onto a line by itself on your post or page, and the player will automaticallly appear.

== Installation ==

1. Install the plugin either from the Wordpress.org plugin directory, or upload the files to your server.
2. Activate the plugin
3. Copy and paste the desired Rewatch video's embed link, on a line by itself
